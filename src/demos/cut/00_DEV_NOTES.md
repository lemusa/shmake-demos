	So in a nutshell the tool is a calculator widget that embeds into your website. this can be embedded inline with your existing content, or as an floating overlay. the widget is customisation to your branding and website styling which i'll touch on shortly. 
	
	as of right now i have 3 modes available, general cuts, fencing and walls. 
	
	the general cut mode allows your potential customers to select the required profile, add their required lengths and then calculate the optimal usage from your stocked product lengths.
	as well as giving you control over what lengths are offered the calculator also allows visitors to limit the maximum length and also filter out any other lengths. you can add multiple products and generate cut lists which is then exportable to a pdf. more on this in a bit,
	
	the next mode is a fencing configurator. users can set the fence length, height and style, then assign product choices to each element to output an entire cutlist to complete the fence. this is then fed into the same cutting calculation algorithm as the general cut mode and output as a complete job cut list, with optional pdf export.
	
	the final current mode is a wall calculator. you can easily draw the wall required with doors and window cutouts to estimate the material required and generate effectively a kitset cut list. you can add multiple wall sections. and again output with option pdf export.
	
	now the pdf exports have configurable options - you can choose to capture the email address of the user, and also give them the option to send their generated cutlist and resulting shoppping list directly to you as a solid leed for your sales team to follow up on. 
	
	behind the widget is a simple admin portal. from here you can view how many visitors to your site have interacted, how many have exported pdfs, and any enquiries to follow up. these can also be emailed straight to your sales inbox. in addition to this you can configure your product catelogue + optional pricing, adjust theme options, and retrieve your embed settings. the embed process is very easy. whoever looks after your website should be able to do it in minutes and im always available to help.
	
	features i plan to add are add-on comsumables like bolts, screws, nails etc. these are easily calculated from the fencing and walls as upsells which not only increase your average cart value but simplify the customer experience. and i'd love to hear your suggestions on what else takes load of your customer service team
